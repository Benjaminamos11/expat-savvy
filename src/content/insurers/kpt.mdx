## About KPT

KPT (CPT) has been serving Swiss residents since 1890, with headquarters in Bern and approximately 450,000 clients nationwide. KPT has established itself as a digital-friendly, modern insurer with a particular focus on telemedicine and online services.

The insurer is especially known for its KPTnet telemedicine service and comprehensive English-language materials, making it an attractive option for internationally-minded professionals and expats who value digital convenience. While KPT has a smaller physical presence than major insurers, their strong digital infrastructure compensates well for tech-savvy customers.
